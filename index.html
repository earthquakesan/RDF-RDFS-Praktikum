What is FOAF? Do I need it?

Is it possible with the current state of the Web?
  • Merge my social networks between various websites
  • Invite my friends from a social media website to a new service I’ve just registered
  • Move the stuff I have on one service to another (e.g. move all my blog posts, comments, etc. from WordPress.com to “Acme Blogs”)

Task 2a: Make an RDFS Description For Your Data

Example

@base <http://iermilov.aksw.org/MyComputer#> .
@prefix rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#> .

os_installed a rdf:Property ;
               rdfs:domain Computer ;
               rdfs:range OS .
You can also use:
  • rdfs:subPropertyOf
  • rdfs:Literal
  • http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#string, #decimal etc.

Task 2a: Making new datatype

Example

@base <http://iermilov.aksw.org/MyComputer#> .
@prefix rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#> .

processing_power a rdf:Property ;
              rdfs:domain Computer ;
              rdfs:range GHz .

GHz a rdf:Property ;
    rdfs:subClassOf <http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#float> .

### In your data you can use it as:
    <http://iermilov.aksw.org/MyComputer> processing_power 1.7^^GHz .

### Which is the same as:
    <http://iermilov.aksw.org/MyComputer> processing_power 1.7^^<http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#float>

Task 2a: Make an RDFS Description For Your Data

RDFS Utility Vocabulary

  • rdfs:seeAlso
    • @base <http://iermilov.aksw.org/MyComputer#> .
@prefix rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#> .

Ubuntu1204 rdfs:seeAlso <http://dbpedia.org/resource/Ubuntu> .
  • rdfs:isDefinedBy
    • rdfs:isDefinedBy is quite similar to rdfs:seeAlso, and it is actually an rdfs:subPropertyOf of rdfs:seeAlso

Ubuntu1204 rdfs:isDefinedBy <http://dbpedia.org/resource/Ubuntu> .
  • rdfs:label
    • Ubuntu1204 rdfs:label "Ubuntu Release 12.04"@en .
  • rdfs:comment
    • Ubuntu1204 rdfs:comment "Ubuntu is the most popular Linux OS."@en .
